Tuna pasta

Description

Canned tuna served with pasta, quick and yummy for your tummy!

Ingredients

  • Canned tuna
  • pasta
  • leek
  • garlic
  • creme fraise
  • chili sauce
  • salt
  • pepper
  • basil

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Chop leek and garlic
  3. Fry leek and garlic
  4. Add tuna to the pan
  5. Add chili sauce and creme fraise to the pan
  6. Add spices to your liking
