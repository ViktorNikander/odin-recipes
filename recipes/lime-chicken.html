Lime Chicken

Description

Fried chicken with lime-juice and zest served with rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Rice
  • Creme fraise
  • Water
  • Soy sauce
  • Honey
  • Lime juice
  • Lime zest
  • Chicken bouillon cube
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Boil rice
  2. Fry chicken
  3. Add everything but parsley to the chicken
  4. Plate rice and lime chicken
  5. Top with parsley
